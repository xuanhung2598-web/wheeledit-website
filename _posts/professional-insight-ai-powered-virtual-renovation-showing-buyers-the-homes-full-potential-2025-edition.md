## What Defines "Premium" Quality?

If you are outsourcing, how do you audit the quality? AI is the engine, but human expertise is the steering wheel. At Wheeledit, we believe the difference between "good" and "sold" lies in the details that AI often misses on its own.

When reviewing your virtual renovations, look for:

1.  **Light Source Consistency:** Does the shadow of the virtual sofa match the direction of the sunlight coming through the window?
2.  **Texture and Grain:** Does the new flooring have the same photographic grain as the rest of the image, or does it look like a smooth 3D render pasted on top?
3.  **Window Pulls and Reflections:** Are the reflections in the virtual mirrors accurate to the room's environment?

These subtleties are what the subconscious mind picks up on. Premium quality feels *real*. It allows the buyer to focus on the home, not the editing.
