What Defines "Premium" AI Quality?
If you are integrating AI tools (as you should be), how do you know if you are getting "premium" results? When marketing homes in 2025, look for these technical markers:

Semantic Window Pulls: Premium AI (like Autoenhance V5.2) understands the difference between a window and a wall. It ensures the view outside is crystal clear and perfectly exposed without making the interior look unnatural—a critical psychological trigger for buyers valuing "light and space."

Geometry Preservation: In architectural rendering, "hallucinations" (bent walls, floating chairs) are deal-breakers. Tools like Veras use geometry overrides to ensure the AI respects the architect's actual lines while generating creative textures.

Contextual Authenticity: Whether it's Adobe Firefly extending a background or ApplyDesign staging a room, the lighting of the inserted objects must match the source light of the room perfectly. Cheap editing misses this; premium editing makes it indistinguishable from reality.
