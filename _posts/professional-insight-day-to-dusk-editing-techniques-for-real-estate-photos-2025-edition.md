## What Defines "Premium" Quality?

Not all edits are created equal. If you are outsourcing or reviewing your current output, you need to know what distinguishes a cheap filter from a premium edit.

High-end Day-to-Dusk editing involves complex techniques that go beyond simply swapping the sky. To maintain realism and curb appeal, a premium edit must include:

1.  **Interior Window Pulls:** The lights inside the house must be turned on virtually, creating a warm glow that spills naturally onto the porch or garden.
2.  **Reflection Management:** If there is a pool or large windows, the new sunset sky must be accurately reflected in those surfaces.
3.  **Color Cast Correction:** A sunset changes the color of the exterior walls. Premium editing adjusts the white balance of the structure to match the ambient light of the new sky, avoiding the "cut-out" look.
4.  **Shadow Logic:** Shadows must align with the position of the virtual sun/moon.
